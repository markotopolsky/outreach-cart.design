# Chuck Richards Knives — lead (EN, odpoveď ako od predajcu)

US nožiar (Kentucky), 116K IG, „Home of the FreeBird", 53 rokov rybár/outdoorsman. Lead #1 z [[outreach-batch-8]] (nože). Web na **Wixe**, drobný katalóg, 7 položiek „Sold out" (overené 20.7.).

## Profil

- **Kto:** Chuck Richards, solo nožiar, signature produkt **FreeBird**
- **Publikum:** 116K IG — najväčšie v nožovej časti batch 8 po @angerknives
- **Web:** chuckrichardsknives.com — Wix, shop s malým katalógom, 7× „Sold out"
- **Uhol:** redesign + demand leak

## Stav konverzácie

**Prvá odpoveď (20.7., ako predajca zákazníkovi):** Nereagoval na otázku „čo sa stane s človekom, ktorý príde a nič nenájde", ale opravil predpoklad: *„The FreeBird is on sale now and available in all options."* — dôkaz hooku.

**Druhá odpoveď (20.7., po priznávaní + reframe):** *„Not one single person says to me they think they're out of stock. Ok. Email to me. Link in bio"*

✅ **Signály:** Chuck akceptuje prístupu (neodmieta), reaguje otvorene, žiada email komunikáciu — **move to email funnel**. Zmena tónu: znižuje defenzívnosť, otvára dvere.

## Komunikácia (IG DM)

**Marko (otvárač, 20.7.):**
> Hey Chuck! 53 years on the water and the FreeBird has basically become its own brand at this point — that's a following most makers never build. Quick question though: I went to the shop to look at what's available and most of it's sold out. When someone lands there on a day like that, what happens to them?

**Chuck Richards (20.7.):**
> The FreeBird is on sale now and available in all options

**Marko (priznanie + reframe, 20.7., odoslané):**
> Appreciate the quick reply, Chuck — I should be straight with you though: I wasn't messaging as a buyer. I build stores for makers.
>
> But that's the thing. I went through the site the way a customer would and came away thinking the FreeBird was gone. You just told me in a DM it's on sale in every option. Everyone landing there today reads it the way I did — and almost none of them will message you to check.
>
> Happy to show you what that looks like fixed if it's ever useful. No pressure.

**Chuck Richards (20.7., reply):**
> Not one single person says to me they think they're out of stock. Ok. Email to me. Link in bio

**Stratégia:**
- **Bez argumentu** — počúva, čo hovorí Chuck, miesto toho, aby mu hovoril čo má.
- **Zdvojenie jeho vety, nie negácia** — „Not one single person says..." sta začína mail. Signalizuje: počujem ťa presne.
- **Otázka, nie tvrdenie** — nechá Chucka vysvetliť, ako to funguje. Ak tam je gap, on ho vidí.
- **Žiadny CTA, žiadne tlak** — len príslub, že ak bude čo ukázať, ukáže. Dôvera.
